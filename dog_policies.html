    Dog Adoption Policies

    Please read our adoption policies below. They are designed to ensure the health and safety of our dogs and the compatibility of the dog(s) with their adoptive family. Note: We ONLY adopt to residents of the Washington metropolitan area, which includes Washington, D.C., and parts of Northern Virginia and Maryland.

    What is the Adoption Fee?

    The adoption fee is $325 per dog.

    • This fee helps cover our costs for medical expenses, spaying/neutering, obedience training and boarding.

    Conditions

    Please note that we will not place a dog in a home if any of the following conditions are present:

    • he prospective home currently has an un-spayed or un-neutered cat or dog.
    • The dog will be left outside when no one is home.
    • The dog is intended as a gift.
    • Puppies less than 6 months old will not be placed in homes:
    • with children under 4 years of age, or
    • where no one is home during the day.

    Once placed, we reserve the right to check on the dog at any time to ensure the dog’s well-being. If any condition of the Adoption Contract has been violated, or if SPCA NOVA believes that the environment is unsafe, we reserve the right to remove the dog from the home.

    If at any time you cannot keep the dog, you must return the dog to the SPCA NOVA and not to another individual, rescue organization or shelter.

    Selection

    It is important that you consider the following:

    • Size – If you rent, does your apartment or home have stated size limits? (Landlord approval is required). Have you considered what size of dog best suits your available space and lifestyle?
    • Age – Dogs of different ages have very different requirements, make sure you know what these are (i.e. puppies and older dogs require extra care and attention).
    • Temperament – Does the dog have a temperament that would be suitable for your family?
    • Compatibility – Is the dog recommended for children and other pets?
    • Energy level – Does your schedule allow for providing the amount of exercise recommended?
    • Special needs – Will the dog require special care?

    Spay/Neuter

    All dogs over the age of 6 months old will be spayed/neutered prior to adoption. If the dog is under the age of 6 months, an additional spay/neuter fee will be charged, and you will be given a certificate for the surgery.

    Obedience Training

    Prior to adoption, our dogs get crate training and learn basic commands, which makes the transition into your home much smoother. We encourage our adopters to continue obedience training.
